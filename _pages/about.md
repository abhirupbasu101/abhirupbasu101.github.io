Hello! I am a math PhD student at the University of Arizona. I work at the [New Frontiers of Sound](https://newfos.arizona.edu/) which is an NSF funded Science and Technology Center focusing on the application of the emerging field of topological acoustics to a wide variety of research areas. My current research focus is on exploring quantum-like phenomena in classical systems and implementing quantum analogue computations on an acoustic platform that uses `phibits' (acoustic analogue of qubits). I have also worked on acoustic topological insulators, multiband topology in acoustic systems and ways to engineer topology in composite systems. My broad interests include nonlinear dynamics in general, information theory and I am currently looking into photonic information processing.
